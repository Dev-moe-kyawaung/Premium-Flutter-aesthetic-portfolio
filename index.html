About Me

Engineer by craft,
builder by nature.

I'm Moe Kyaw Aung, a Senior Android Developer and Full Stack Engineer with over 12 years of hands-on experience building mobile and backend software that scales.

My core work lives in Kotlin and Flutter — writing production apps with Jetpack Compose, architecting backends with Ktor on Azure, and pushing CI/CD pipelines through GitHub Actions. I believe great software is built at the intersection of clean architecture and obsessive attention to UX detail.

Outside of client work, I actively explore AI/ML integration (particularly Claude API and Azure Cognitive Services) and cybersecurity/ethical hacking. I hold a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Computer Studies, Pathein, and a BA in English from Pathein Distance University — a combination that makes me as comfortable in a design review as in a code review.

Based between Tachileik, Myanmar and Bangkok, Thailand. Open to remote collaboration worldwide.
